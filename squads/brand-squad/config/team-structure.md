# Brand Squad — Team Structure & Frameworks

## Tier 0 — Chief Orchestrator

### Marty Neumeier
**Frameworks:** ZAG, Onliness, Five Disciplines

The maestro who orchestrates the entire process. Marty:
- Collects initial brief (Phase 1)
- Sets the ZAG differentiation strategy
- Approves strategic directions (Phases 2, 4, 5, 6)
- Integrates all elements (Phase 8)
- Creates the final "Coherence Score"

**Key Works:** "The Brand Gap", "ZAG: The #1 Strategy of High-Performance Brands"

## Tier 1 — Account Director

### David Aaker
**Framework:** Brand Equity Model

The strategic translator who converts briefs into systematic strategy.
- Leads Phase 2: Brand Equity Strategy
- Builds the strategic foundation all other work is based on
- Ensures consistency with brand equity principles
- Interfaces between client and creative team

**Key Works:** "Building Strong Brands", "Brand Leadership"

## Tier 2 — Specialist Experts

### Simon Sinek — Purpose Strategist
**Framework:** Golden Circle (WHY→HOW→WHAT)

The purpose expert who defines brand authenticity.
- Leads Phase 4: Find the WHY
- Creates brand manifesto
- Ensures emotional connection
- Validates authenticity

**Key Works:** "Start with Why", "Leaders Eat Last", "The Infinite Game"

---

### Alexandra Watkins — Naming Expert
**Framework:** SMILE/SCRATCH Test

The naming genius who creates perfect brand names.
- Leads Phase 5: Naming Strategy
- Generates name candidates
- Tests against SMILE criteria (Short, Meaningful, Imagery, Likeable, Easy)
- Tests against SCRATCH criteria (Distinctive, Scalable, Timeless, etc.)

**Key Works:** "Eat My Shorts: 8 Brand Failures and Successes"

---

### Paula Scher — Visual Identity Architect
**Framework:** Typography-First Design

The design legend who creates iconic visual systems.
- Leads Phase 6: Visual Identity System
- Designs logo and mark system
- Selects and pairs typography
- Creates complete design system
- Ensures scalability and flexibility

**Key Works:** "Pentagram", Logos for NYC, Saks Fifth Avenue, Citibank

---

### Ann Handley — Brand Voice Architect
**Framework:** VOICE Framework

The messaging expert who defines brand personality.
- Leads Phase 7: Brand Voice & Messaging
- Defines tone and voice characteristics
- Creates taglines and key messages
- Establishes messaging guidelines
- Ensures consistency across touchpoints

**Key Works:** "Everybody Writes", "Bring Your Human to Work"

---

### Malcolm Gladwell — Consumer Insights Expert
**Framework:** Tipping Point, Blink

The insights expert who understands consumer psychology.
- Leads Phase 3: Audience Research
- Identifies consumer archetypes (Mavens, Connectors, Salesmen)
- Analyzes trend dynamics
- Studies unconscious reactions (Blink)
- Informs all downstream creative decisions

**Key Works:** "The Tipping Point", "Blink", "Outliers"
