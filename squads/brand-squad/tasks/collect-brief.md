# collect-brief — Initial Client Briefing Collection

**Task ID:** collect-brief
**Agent Responsible:** @account-manager (Priya Patel)
**Phase:** 1 - Discovery
**Duration:** 2-3 days
**Status:** Ready

## Purpose

Collect comprehensive briefing information from client to inform all downstream branding decisions. This is the foundation for strategy and creative work.

## Pre-Conditions

- [ ] Client contact established
- [ ] Kickoff meeting scheduled
- [ ] Team availability confirmed

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Completed briefing document with all required sections
- [ ] Client feedback and approvals collected
- [ ] Briefing shared with entire team
- [ ] No critical gaps in information

## Steps

### Step 1: Pre-Meeting Preparation

Prepare briefing template and review any available client materials.

### Step 2: Discovery Meeting

Conduct comprehensive discovery meeting covering:
- Company history and background
- Product/service overview
- Target audiences
- Business objectives
- Competitive landscape
- Brand perception
- Timeline and budget

### Step 3: Documentation

Document all findings in standardized briefing template.

### Step 4: Internal Review

Present briefing to Brand Strategist for gaps identification.

### Step 5: Finalization

Finalize briefing document and share with all team members.

## Deliverables

- Completed Briefing Document
- Supporting materials and references
- Client approval email
